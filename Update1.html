The Pinewood and Campolindo devices are back up!

Last week we visited Pinewood School and Campolindo High School to update our devices and improve the mechanical stability to restore reliable data-taking. We had fantastic help from students at both schools, with special thanks to Mrs. Abraham at Pinewood and Ms. Eaton at Campolindo for helping us arrange our visits!
