Alameda High Science and Engineering clubs visit

March 18th we had a visit from the Alameda High School Science and Engineering clubs. We had a great time showing the students around our RadWatch and DoseNet lab area, talking to them about some of the cutting edge research being conducted by our group. The students had some great questions and we're really looking forward to working with these clubs more in the future!
