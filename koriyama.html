DoseNet has become an international network, with our 6th device now posting data from Koriyama City, Japan!

During the 2nd International Symposium for Resilient Communities two members of our DoseNet team installed a device at City Hall in Koriyama City, Japan. We are thrilled to have Koriyama city as part of our network and we're looking forward to building on this new connection.
